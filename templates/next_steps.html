I'm New

Find service times, directions, and what to expect on your first visit

Welcome! We're so glad you're considering visiting CAAC Church. Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a new church home, we want your first visit to be comfortable and meaningful.

Your First Visit

1
Plan Your Visit

Check our service times and location. Sunday services are at 9:15 AM (English) and 10:00 AM (Mandarin & Cantonese). We're located at ISAACS Community Center with plenty of parking available.

2
What to Expect

Our services typically last about 90 minutes and include worship music, biblical teaching, and time for community connection. Dress comfortably - you'll see everything from casual to business casual attire.

3
For Your Family

We have excellent programs for children and youth during our services. Our nursery cares for infants and toddlers, while children ages 3-12 enjoy age-appropriate Bible lessons and activities.

Service Details

English Service: Sundays at 9:15 AM - Contemporary worship with relevant biblical teaching for all ages.

Mandarin Service: Sundays at 10:00 AM - Worship and teaching in Mandarin for Chinese-speaking community.

Cantonese Service: Sundays at 10:00 AM - Traditional worship and fellowship in Cantonese.

When you arrive, look for our welcome team wearing "Welcome" badges. They'll help you find your way around, introduce you to others, and answer any questions you might have.

Get Connected

Grow in your faith and find community through our life groups and ministries

Real life change happens in the context of relationships. Getting connected means finding your place in our church family and growing together with others on the same journey.

Ways to Connect

1
Join a Life Group

Small groups that meet weekly in homes for Bible study, prayer, and friendship. We have groups for all ages and stages of life.

2
Attend a Newcomers Lunch

Monthly gatherings where you can meet our pastors and other new attendees in a casual setting.

3
Explore Our Ministries

From men's and women's ministries to youth programs and senior fellowship, there's a place for everyone.

Ministry Opportunities

Men's Ministry: Monthly breakfasts, Bible studies, and service projects that build strong Christian men.

Women's Ministry: Weekly Bible studies, retreats, and special events designed to encourage and equip women.

Youth Ministry: Dynamic programs for middle and high school students including weekly gatherings, camps, and mission trips.

Young Adults: Community for those in their 20s and 30s navigating career, relationships, and faith.

Seniors Fellowship: Monthly luncheons, day trips, and service opportunities for our mature members.

The best way to get started is to fill out a connection card on Sunday or contact our church office. We'll help you find the right group or ministry based on your interests and stage of life.

Serve Others

Discover your gifts and make a difference by serving in and outside the church

Every member is a minister. Serving is how we live out our faith and make a real difference in our church, community, and world.

How to Get Started Serving

1
Discover Your Gifts

Take our spiritual gifts assessment to understand how God has uniquely equipped you to serve.

2
Explore Opportunities

Learn about the various serving teams and ministries where you can make a difference.

3
Start Serving

Join a serving team that matches your gifts, passions, and availability.

Serving Opportunities

Sunday Service Teams: Welcome team, audio/visual, worship team, children's ministry, hospitality.

Community Outreach: Food pantry volunteers, neighborhood clean-up, visiting nursing homes, ESL teachers.

Behind the Scenes: Administration, facility maintenance, graphic design, website support.

Global Missions: Short-term mission trips, supporting missionaries, prayer teams.

You don't need special qualifications to start serving - just a willing heart. We provide training and support for every role. The best way to begin is to try out a serving opportunity that interests you.

We believe that everyone has a next step to take in their relationship with God and involvement in the church. Whether you're just starting to explore faith or you're ready to dive deep into serving, we're here to help you take that next step.
