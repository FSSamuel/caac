Our Mission

To lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ.

We are a community of believers committed to sharing God's love through worship, discipleship, and service. Our doors are open to everyone, regardless of where you are on your spiritual journey.

Our Core Beliefs

The foundation of our faith

The Bible

We believe the Bible is God's inspired Word, the final authority for faith and life.

Jesus Christ

We believe in Jesus Christ as God's Son and humanity's only Savior through His death and resurrection.

The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit empowers believers for godly living and service.

Our History

Over 50 years of ministry in Canberra

1970 - Founding

CAAC Church was founded with just 12 members meeting in a small rented space.

1985 - First Building

We purchased and renovated our first permanent location, allowing us to expand our ministries.

2002 - Current Campus

We moved to our current campus, featuring a 500-seat sanctuary and education wing.

Today - Thriving Community

With multiple congregations, we continue to grow and serve the Canberra community through worship services and outreach programs.
