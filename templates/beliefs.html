Our Core Beliefs

The foundation of our faith and practice

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the final authority for faith and life.

"All Scripture is God-breathed..."
— 2 Timothy 3:16

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

"Therefore go and make disciples..."
— Matthew 28:19

Salvation

We believe salvation comes by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by works.

"For it is by grace you have been saved..."
— Ephesians 2:8-9

The Church

We believe the Church is Christ's body on earth, called to worship, fellowship, and evangelism.

"And let us consider how we may spur..."
— Hebrews 10:24-25

Second Coming

We believe in the personal, visible return of Jesus Christ to establish His eternal kingdom.

"He will come again in glory..."
— Acts 1:11

Christian Living

We believe Christians are called to holiness, service, and love for one another.

"Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart..."
— Colossians 3:23
